Crystal Canyon Temple

The four great gems in the Crystal Canyon Temple are protected by a spell holding them in place. The instructions seemed easy enough, but that hadn't stopped countless hours wasted before. Link had learned that nothing in the temples and dungeons of Hyrule ever comes easy.

It seemed as though when he stood on the magic panel a number lit up in the rock holding the gems. When he touched the gems a second number would appear, and grow in different increments based on the gem touched.
