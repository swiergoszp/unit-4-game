A "Strange" Encounter

The strength of the Infinity Stones are unrivaled. Things of legend, they are said to be the remnants of forces that existed even before the creation of our universe.

Dr. Strange, the Sorcerer Supreme, has managed to locate all six of them. However in order to contain them and keep them hidden, their power must be nullified. Luckily, Strange has devised a strategy to use the stones against one another to supress their strength.

Each stone vibrates at a different frequency from the others. Using these different vibrational patterns, Strange believes he can create layers of magic barriers to do the trick. This is where you come in...

In order to discover the frequency and how much energy it contains, you mush interact with stones. Dr. Strange had provided you with a magical set of blacksmith tong he calls the "Pincers of Rathalos". Using this artifact allows Strange the time he needs to add the stones power to his spell.
